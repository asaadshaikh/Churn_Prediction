Customer Churn Prediction

This application predicts customer churn for a subscription-based business. By identifying customers who are likely to cancel their subscriptions, businesses can take proactive measures to retain them.

How it works

Our model analyzes various customer attributes such as:

  • Usage patterns
  • Billing information
  • Contract details
  • Service subscriptions
  • Customer demographics
  • Support interactions
Our machine learning model has been trained on historical data and achieves high accuracy in identifying at-risk customers.
